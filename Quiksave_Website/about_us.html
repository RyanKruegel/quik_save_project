ABOUT US!

"We hope you enjoy QuikSave! We created this idea after many years of scanning through YouTube, exploring all the different types of gaming content we love.. but, we found a good niche that we thought we could contribute to. We wanted to be video game historians!" - Ryan K


Ryan (24) & Justin (20) are the founders of QuikSave Productions. They have always been fans of games since the age of 5. Ryan's first game was Halo all the way back in 2001. Without it, he may have never fell in love with this vast medium, like he is today! Justin was always a fan of fighting games... Mortal Kombat, Street Fighter, Tekken. Together they had the idea to spawn a YouTube channel and brand to share their love of older games that're no longer appreciated by the masses.
